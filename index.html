    How do I get there?

    From the French Quarter, the venue is approximately 15-20 minutes away via car (a Lyft will usually cost $7-10), or you can enjoy a scenic ferry ride across the Mississippi River to Algiers Point, where you can take a short walk to the venue.

    Where do I park?

    What's the weather going to be like?

    Usually in late March, New Orleans has high temperatures in the mid-seventies and lows around 60. Generally March isn't a particularlly rainy time of year, but rain in New Orleans is common and often unpredictable! In the event of rain the evening of our wedding, we will move the festivities indoors.

    Where should I stay while I'm in New Orleans?

    If you're traveling from out of town, there are tons of places to stay in New Orleans, so you'll have many options to choose from! Be aware that March is a very popular time to visit our city, so we recommend making travel plans & hotel reservations as far in advance as possible.

    We would highly suggest a hotel in the French Quarter, as there are many attractions, bars & restaurants to visit while you're in town. From the French Quarter, the venue is approximately 15-20 minutes away via car (a Lyft will usually cost $7-10), or you can enjoy a scenic ferry ride across the Mississippi River to Algiers Point, where you can take a short walk to the venue.
