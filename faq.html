FAQ



What should I wear?

We encourage you to dress in garden party attire! Please be aware that the venue is largely a grass courtyard, so you'll want to avoid heels! It may get chilly in the evening, so be prepared!


How do I get there?

From the French Quarter, the venue is approximately 15-20 minutes away via car (a Lyft will usually cost $7-10), or you can enjoy a scenic ferry ride across the Mississippi River to Algiers Point, where you can take a short walk to the venue.
More info on the ferry here


Where do I park?

There's street parking in front of Compass Point, as well as along the back side of the property. We're working on creating a map to help out with parking!


What's the weather going to be like?

Usually in late March, New Orleans has high temperatures in the mid-seventies and lows around 60. Generally March isn't a particularlly rainy time of year, but rain in New Orleans is common and often unpredictable!


What time should I arrive?

We plan on starting the ceremony at 6:30 p.m., so we ask you to arrive about 15 minutes early so you'll be seated when we get started


Are kids welcome?

Yes! We're looking forward to seeing your whole family!


What kind of food will be served?

We're still working on finalizing the menu, but you can expect classic New Orleans cuisine.
